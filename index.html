Australia has one of the highest cancer survival rates in the world. This means that cancer patient health must be closely monitored to not only improve individual patients’ wellbeing, but to also gain valuable data about the effectiveness of cancer treatments.

PatientPal

PatientPal is a survey toolkit that we’ve been developing for Didymo Designs and NSW Health. Our software will be used by both medical personnel to create surveys for tracking cancer patients’ progress, as well as by patients to complete surveys and send them back.

Improved Toolkit

PatientPal will serve as a replacement for PROSAIQ. The survey toolkit built with KoboToolbox that is currently used, incorporates various features of PROSAIQ’s design features and flows into our software foundation.
